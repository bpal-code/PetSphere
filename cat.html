Know More About Your Cat 💖

Choose Your Cat

Persian Cat

Short Description:
Long-haired, calm, and very affectionate — one of the most loved indoor cats.
Care Tips:
• Daily coat brushing to avoid tangles
• Keep indoors (they are sensitive to heat)
• Clean eyes regularly
• Feed high-quality hairball-control food

Scottish Fold

Short Description:
Known for its folded ears and soft, gentle personality.
Care Tips:
• Use soft bristle brush
• Avoid over-exercising (joint issues)
• Provide warm sleeping spots
• Balanced diet with supplements if needed

Siamese Cat

Short Description:
Vocal, intelligent, and very social. Loves human attention.
Care Tips:
• Needs daily playtime
• Provide interactive toys
• Keep mentally stimulated
• Clean ears weekly

Maine Coon

Short Description:
One of the largest cat breeds — gentle, fluffy, and friendly.
Care Tips:
• Weekly brushing
• Larger litter box required
• Provide climbing space
• Feed high-protein diet

Bengal Cat

Short Description:
Wild-looking coat, energetic personality, and very playful.
Care Tips:
• Need lots of exercise
• Cat trees and climbing shelves
• Provide puzzle toys
• Bathing occasionally helps with shedding

Ragdoll

Short Description:
Soft, fluffy, and very calm — known for going “limp” when picked up.
Care Tips:
• Regular brushing
• Keep indoors for safety
• Needs gentle play
• Routine vet checkups

British Shorthair

Short Description:
Chubby-cheeked, quiet, and easy-going family cat.
Care Tips:
• Minimal grooming
• Controlled diet (prone to obesity)
• Keep active with toys
• Brush weekly

Indian Cat

Short Description:
Strong, healthy, low-maintenance cats commonly found in India.
Care Tips:
• Very little grooming
• Regular vaccinations
• Provide scratching posts
• Feed clean, balanced diet
